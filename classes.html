Couples Pottery Class

Rediscover connection and creativity with our Couples Pottery Class. Whether you're on a first date or celebrating years of love, this hands-on session is designed to bring you closer together—literally! Learn the basics of wheel throwing and hand building side-by-side as you create something meaningful and fun. Our instructors guide you through every step, ensuring laughter, teamwork, and maybe even a little mess. By the end of the session, you'll both walk away with handmade pieces and unforgettable memories.
